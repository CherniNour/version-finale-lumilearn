Our Mission 💖

We believe that every child deserves a joyful and accessible education. No matter where you come from, what language you speak, or how you learn, LumiLearn is here for you.

Our mission is simple but powerful: to transform learning into a colorful adventure where every child can shine in their own way. Because we know that you are not "slow" or "different" – you are unique, and that's your greatest strength ! 🌈

Our Team

We are a group of passionate students from ENSIT and ENSI, united by a common vision: to make education accessible and joyful for all children. Through the IEEE CS × SIGHT Group project, we combine our skills in computer science, design, and pedagogy to create LumiLearn.
